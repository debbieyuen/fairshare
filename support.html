Support

Union — help without signing in

This page is for App Store support and anyone who needs help using Union. You do not need an account to read it.

Get the app

Open Union on your iPhone or Android device from the store listing, or use the web app at https://app.fairshare.social/.

Contact us

  • General questions: hello@fairshare.social
  • Safety, abuse, or illegal content: abuse@fairshare.social
In-app safety. You can report messages and users and block users from inside Union (contact details and chat). Reports are reviewed promptly.

Account & data

  • Delete your account: Profile → Delete Account → confirm. This permanently removes your account and associated data.
  • Privacy: See our Privacy Policy.
  • Terms: See our Terms of Use.

Common topics

  • Sign-up: Union uses an invite-based flow. If someone shared a link or QR code with you, use that to join.
  • Group credits: In-app tokens are fictional social points for groups only; they are not money and cannot be cashed out.
