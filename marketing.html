Union

Keep track of people you actually meet. Build trust in small groups.

Union is a social app for real-world relationships: exchange contact info with QR codes, stay in touch, vouch for people you trust, and run lightweight group “credits” for community coordination. Optional features include chat and location sharing with people you choose.

What you can do

  • Meet someone and swap details quickly with QR codes.
  • See mutual connections and grow your network safely.
  • Form groups with an internal social ledger (not real money).
  • Message people you are connected to; report or block when needed.

Try Union — install from the App Store or open the web app. For help, see Support, Privacy, and Terms.
