Privacy Policy

Union — Last updated May 1, 2026

1. What Union Is

Union is a social app that lets people manage their contacts, build trust, and form groups. It is available as a web app and as a native iOS app. This policy covers both.

2. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign up we collect your email address, a display name you choose, and a password. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are never stored in plain text.

Profile information. You may optionally add a phone number and a profile photo. You control whether your phone number or email is shared with individual contacts.

Photos. When you meet a new contact you may take a selfie together. Photos you upload (profile picture, contact selfies, group logos) are stored in cloud storage and may be visible to other Union users you interact with.

Location. Union requests location access for two optional features: discovering nearby contacts and sharing a map pin in group chat. Your coordinates are stored temporarily on our server to check for nearby contacts and are not retained as a location history. Map pin locations you share in chat are visible to other members of that group.

Messages. Text messages you send in group chat are stored on our server and visible to members of that group.

Push notification tokens. If you enable push notifications, we store the device token needed to deliver notifications to your device. On the web this is a Web Push subscription; on iOS this is an APNs device token.

Usage data. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To connect you with other Union users you choose to interact with
  • To deliver push notifications you have opted into
  • To show nearby contacts when you enable location features
  • To display your profile to other users within the app

4. Third-Party Services

Union relies on a small number of infrastructure services to operate:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage, and serverless functions
  • Carto / OpenStreetMap — map tiles and reverse geocoding for location features
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — push delivery on iOS
  • Web Push (browser vendor infrastructure) — push delivery on the web

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Sharing With Other Users

Union is a social app, so some information is visible to other users by design:

  • Your display name and profile photo are visible to other Union users
  • Your phone number and email are shared only with contacts you explicitly choose to share them with
  • Messages and locations you share in a group chat are visible to all members of that group
  • Photos taken during a contact handshake are visible to both parties

6. Safety and Moderation

Union has zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users. Every user can report objectionable content and block other users from inside the app. Reports are reviewed and acted on within 24 hours. Users who post objectionable content or behave abusively will be removed and may have their accounts terminated.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Location data used for the nearby feature is short-lived and overwritten on each update.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the app: open Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You can also email us at the address below if you prefer to request deletion that way.

8. Security

Communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit via TLS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use row-level security policies in our database so users can only access data they are authorized to see.

9. Minors

Union is rated 17+ and is intended for adults. By creating an account you confirm you are at least 17 years old. Union is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

For users between 13 and 17 in jurisdictions where parental consent is required, a parent or legal guardian should review this policy and the safety features described above (in-app reporting, blocking, location-share opt-in per contact, account deletion). Parents who believe their child has provided personal information without consent can email privacy@fairshare.social and we will delete the account and associated data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of Union after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, email us at privacy@fairshare.social.
